# Virtual Dreamachine

> *"Dreaming can be done with the eyes open."*
> — Brion Gysin

A browser-based, stereoscopic Dreamachine built for VR headsets — inspired by the original flickering light sculpture created by **Brion Gysin** and **Ian Sommerville** in 1961, championed by **William S. Burroughs** as the first art object to be experienced with the eyes closed.

**[→ Enter the Dreamachine](https://myathemagick-netizen.github.io/virtual-dreammachine/)**

---

## ⚠ Epilepsy Warning

**This experience produces rapid, rhythmic flickering light between 1 and 30 Hz designed to fill your entire field of vision.**

Do not use if you have photosensitive epilepsy, are prone to seizures, or are sensitive to flashing light. Stop immediately if you feel discomfort, nausea, or dizziness. A warning screen is shown before the experience begins.

---

## What is a Dreamachine?

The original Dreamachine was a slitted paper cylinder rotating around a light bulb at 78 RPM. Viewed with eyes closed, the flickering light at alpha and theta frequencies (roughly 8–13 Hz) induces vivid hypnagogic visions — geometric patterns, colours, and dream-like imagery generated entirely by the viewer's own nervous system.

Gysin and Burroughs considered it the first artwork to be experienced from the inside.

This version brings that experience into the browser and VR, using a stereoscopic side-by-side canvas that can be viewed in a headset or cardboard viewer.

## How to Use

1. Open the link above in a browser — or load it in your VR headset's browser in side-by-side stereo mode
2. Read and acknowledge the epilepsy warning
3. Sit close to the screen, level with your eyes, in a darkened room
4. **Close your eyes** — the light passes through your eyelids
5. Allow a few minutes for the visions to develop

## Brainwave States

The flicker frequency slider moves through four brainwave states:

| State | Frequency | Quality |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| Delta | 1–4 Hz | Deep, slow, oceanic |
| **Theta** | **4–8 Hz** | **Hypnagogic — the richest vision state** |
| Alpha | 8–13 Hz | Gysin's original target — relaxed imagery |
| Beta | 13–30 Hz | Alert, sharp, geometric |

Theta (4–8 Hz) is recommended for beginners. This is the frequency range of the hypnagogic state — the threshold between waking and sleep where the most vivid dreamlike visions occur.

## Patterns

- **Radial slits** — closest to the original rotating cylinder geometry
- **Mandala** — petal forms that generate interference patterns
- **Vortex** — spiral arms producing depth and motion
- **Pulsing rings** — concentric waves of light

## VR Usage

The experience renders in **side-by-side stereoscopic** format. To use with a headset:

- Load the URL in your headset's browser (Meta Browser, Firefox Reality, etc.)
- Switch to side-by-side / stereo mode in your headset settings
- Or use a Google Cardboard-style viewer held up to a phone screen
