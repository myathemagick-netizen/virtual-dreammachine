Dreamachine
After Brion Gysin & William S. Burroughs · 1961

This experience produces rapid rhythmic flickering light between 1–30 Hz designed to fill your field of vision. It can trigger seizures in people with photosensitive epilepsy. Do not use if you are prone to seizures. Stop immediately if you feel discomfort or dizziness.

Close your eyes once inside — the light passes through your eyelids and the visions are your own.
