⚠  Photosensitive Epilepsy Warning  ⚠

Dreamachine
After Brion Gysin & Ian Sommerville · 1961

This experience produces rapid rhythmic flickering light between 1–30 Hz filling your field of vision. It can trigger seizures in people with photosensitive epilepsy. Do not use if prone to seizures. Stop immediately if you feel discomfort.

Sit down before entering. Close your eyes — the light passes through your eyelids.
