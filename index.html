Dreamachine
After Brion Gysin & William S. Burroughs, 1961

This experience produces rapid rhythmic flickering light at frequencies between 1 and 30 Hz, designed to fill your entire field of vision. This can trigger seizures in people with photosensitive epilepsy. Do not use if you are prone to seizures or sensitive to flashing lights. Stop immediately if you feel discomfort, nausea, or dizziness.

For the authentic Dreamachine experience — sit close, hold the screen at eye level, and close your eyes. The light passes through your eyelids and induces the visions.
