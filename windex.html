Dreamachine
After Brion Gysin & William S. Burroughs, 1961

This experience produces rapid rhythmic flickering light at frequencies between 1 and 30 Hz. It is designed to fill your field of vision and induce hypnagogic imagery through brainwave entrainment. This can trigger seizures in people with photosensitive epilepsy. Do not use if you are prone to seizures or sensitive to flashing lights. Stop immediately if you feel discomfort, nausea, or dizziness.

Sit or stand comfortably. The machine will appear before you in the dark. Close your eyes — the light passes through your eyelids and the visions are your own.
